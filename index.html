The Problem

SA Businesses Are Drowning in Fragmented Banking

Most South African companies bank with multiple institutions — but there's no unified view, no consistent approval process, and no single source of truth.

🔀 Fragmented Cash Visibility

Treasury teams log into 3–5 separate banking portals just to see their total cash position — wasting hours and increasing risk of errors.

⚠️ Inconsistent Approval Processes

Each bank has different approval workflows. High-value payments slip through without proper multi-level sign-off or audit trails.

📊 No Unified Reporting

Building consolidated cash reports requires manual exports from every bank — an error-prone, time-consuming process with no real-time accuracy.

Security Architecture

Enterprise-Grade Security
for South African Treasury

OneBank SA is built with security at every layer — from bcrypt-hashed passwords and JWT sessions to API-level role enforcement and a complete audit trail.

Security Controls

Defence in Depth

Multiple independent security layers ensure your treasury data is protected at rest, in transit, and at the application level.

🔑
Authentication

NextAuth.js v5 with credentials provider. JWT tokens with server-side validation. Session-based authentication with secure cookie handling.

NextAuth.js v5

🔒
Password Security

All passwords are hashed using bcrypt with cost factor 12 — the industry standard. Plain-text passwords are never stored or logged anywhere in the system.

bcrypt cost factor 12

🛡️
Route Protection

proxy.ts middleware enforces authentication on all dashboard routes. requireAuth() and requireRole() guards protect every sensitive API endpoint.

Centralised enforcement

🌐
Transport Security

TLS/HTTPS enforced by Vercel. HTTP requests automatically redirect to HTTPS. Neon PostgreSQL database connection requires SSL (sslmode=require).

TLS enforced

📋
Security Headers

HSTS (max-age=63072000), X-Frame-Options: SAMEORIGIN, X-Content-Type-Options: nosniff, Referrer-Policy, Permissions-Policy, and DNS-Prefetch-Control — all applied via next.config.ts.

OWASP hardened

📝
Complete Audit Trail

ActivityLog model tracks every system event — action type, description, user name, entity, IP address, status, and full timestamp. Searchable and filterable in the admin UI.

Full audit log

⏱️
Rate Limiting

Upstash Redis-backed rate limiting on authentication endpoints — 10 requests per 10 seconds per IP. Prevents brute force attacks in production with graceful local fallback.

Upstash Redis

💰
Transfer Limits

Role-based payment amount restrictions enforced at API level — daily, per-transaction, and monthly limits configurable per user role to prevent unauthorised high-value payments.

Role-enforced

📱
QR Pay Authentication

Configurable authentication method for QR payments — choose from Biometric, PIN, Password, or OTP. Security level indicator shows risk rating per method selected.

Configurable

OneBank SA processes personal and financial data in accordance with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act, Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA).

Data We Process: Name, email address, role, bank account information, transaction data, and audit logs.

Lawful Basis: Legitimate business interest for treasury management purposes.

Purpose Limitation: Data is collected solely for the purpose of treasury management and is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Subject Rights: You have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your personal information. Contact bongimsk80@gmail.com to exercise these rights.
